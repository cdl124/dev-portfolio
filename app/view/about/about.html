About Cortney

A curious mind, an eye for design, a penchant for technology.

It all started at a young age, drawing all my thoughts and feelings onto scraps of paper, using any writing utensil I could find. My creations quickly went digital as I was introduced to Microsoft Paint on the family PC, and got more advanced several years later with Photoshop and HTML. From there, I turned my love for art and technology into a hobby in graphic design, and now, a rising career in full-stack web development and software engineering.

My projects and work reflect my passion for minimalist, aesthetic design that makes app navigation productive and stress-free. No interface should ever leave me guessing, and I expect the same in all the apps I create.

You may find me hitting the slopes in the winter, practicing my limited parkour skills year-round, or drawing in my sketchbook at home.
